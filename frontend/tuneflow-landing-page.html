How TuneFlow Works

A simple process to revolutionize how music is shared, owned, and monetized.

1

Artist Uploads Music

Artists upload both low and high-quality versions of their music to the platform, specifying royalty percentages for all contributors.

2

Mint NFT

The platform mints an NFT with metadata including the song details, contributor information, and royalty splits.

3

User Listens & Purchases

Fans can listen to low-quality previews for free, then purchase the NFT to unlock high-quality streaming and exclusive content.

4

Automatic Royalties

Smart contracts instantly distribute payments to all contributors based on pre-set percentages on every sale or stream.

5

Performance Tracking

Artists can view real-time data on streams, sales, and royalties through their performance dashboard.

6

Community Governance

Token holders can stake and vote on platform changes and improvements, creating a truly artist-owned ecosystem.
